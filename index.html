Apply blending to an image. The adjustment "layer" can be either an image, or a solid color

These are designed to match Photoshop's blend modes as closely as possible.

Download: https://raw.github.com/farski/jquery-blend/master/jquery.blend.min.js

      $('.color').blend(mode: 'overlay', adjustment: 'rgb(67, 94, 213)');
      img src="image.jpg" class="color"
      
      $('.image').blend(mode: 'overlay', adjustment: 'otherimage.jpg');
      img src="image.jpg" class="image"
